## About the dashboard

The HR manager required a dashboard for the workers working in the company. She needed an interactive dashboard that have:
      An overview:
         An overview that had the following;
           1. The total number of hired employees, active employees, and terminated employees.
           2. A visual of the total number of hired and terminated employees over the years.
           3. A breakdown of total employees by department and job titles.
           4. A comparison of the total employees between headquarters (HQ) and branches (Nairobi as the HQ)
           5. Show the distribution of employees by city and state. 
       Demographics section:
         A demographic section that had the following;
           1. gender ratio in the company.
           2. A visual of the distribution of employees across age groups and education levels. 
           4. Total number of employees within each age group.
           5. Total number of employees within each education level.
           6.  correlation between employeesâ€™s educational backgrounds and their 
              performance ratings.
       Income section:
           1. Comparison of salaries across different education levels for both genders to identify any 
               discrepancies or patterns.
            2. Presentation of how the age correlate with the salary for employees in each department.
